At my home

I live in a small town in Ukraine in the Lviv region. I live near the city center in a small apartment. Very convenient (close to all the city events in the center, schools and other institutions I visit. My parents live in the apartment with me. There are also three parrots, a turtle, fish. And we recently bought a Yorkshire Terrier dog. Some say it's almost a zoo , but it is a great responsibility that my parents and I take in. I love my animals, I love my town where everyone knows each other and chews peacefully. I love my life and try to make it more productive and better.
